How BadgeFed Works

1. Become a Certifier

Join BadgeFed as a certifier and start issuing digital badges. Certifiers play a crucial role in recognizing and validating achievements within the community. Learn more about becoming a certifier here.

2. Issue Digital Badges

Organizations can design and distribute digital badges to recognize achievements, skills, and milestones, adhering to the Open Badge Standard for interoperability. Learn more about digital badges here.

3. Earn and Share Badges

Users earn badges through verified accomplishments and can share them across various platforms within the Fediverse, enhancing their professional profiles and social presence. Explore Fediverse platforms here.

4. Verify Credentials

Utilize BadgeFed's decentralized verification system to authenticate badges, ensuring credibility and trust within the network. Simplify the credentialing process with our user-friendly interface.
